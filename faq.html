FAQ

Is there a dedicated inbox for specific enquiries regarding workouts and/or nutrition?

If you have any questions or problems please look through all FAQ below and you will find your answer. If you are still experiencing any issues please contact us via the support center or DM me via Instagram @jemmifit. Thank You!

Do you recommend a specific brand for supplements and/or equipment?

I use supplements by Women’s Best. It’s completely up to you whether you want to use supplements or not, but from my personal experience it is better to trust well known brands. If you don’t want to use any supplements, that is perfectly fine.

Does this guide include a personalised nutrition plan?

No, this is only a workout guide and doesn’t include a personalized nutrition plan.

How do I know if this guide is suitable for me?

This guide is suitable for those at beginner to intermediate fitness levels. As a beginner, you should simply use lighter weights.

*If you are ill, pregnant, feel pain or injured, please consult your doctor beforehand.

How many workouts (or meals) per week are included?

4 workouts per week. Focused on developing your glutes and legs but also your upper body and core.

How many weeks does it take to complete the guide?

8 weeks. You can repeat the guide once you have completed it.

Has this guide been designed by you?

This guide is based on the training I do myself and helped me to achieve my goals at the gym.

What’s included when I buy this guide?

You gain access to the AFLETE app and video instructions for all exercises.

What equipment do I need for this guide?

My guide is gym based (you just need access to standard machines and free weights).

Do I have to follow this guide exactly as it was created?

If you want or need to change something you can and will can get perfect results. This program has helped me, so if you want to follow my workouts I can recommend to follow this guide exactly as it was created.
